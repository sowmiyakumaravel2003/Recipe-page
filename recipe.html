Chicken Briyani
  • Oil
  • Amul butter
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Chicken
  • Spices
  • BasmatiRice
  • Curd
  • Salt
  • Mint leaves
Procedure

Marinate chicken with yogurt, spices, and salt. Wash and partially cook basmati rice with spices. Sauté onions until golden, set some aside for garnish. Cook marinated chicken until it changes color. Add green chilies, cumin seeds, and bay leaves. Layer partially cooked rice over the chicken. Sprinkle mint and coriander leaves on top. saffron milk mixture (optional) for color. Cover, cook on low for 20-25 minutes. Garnish with fried onions, serve hot. Enjoy!

Gulab Jamun
  • Milk Powder
  • Flour
  • Ghee
  • Baking soda
  • Yogurt
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Cardamom
  • saffron
Procedure

Gather all the required incrediants to make. Combine milk powder, flour, and baking soda in a bowl. Mix in ghee and yogurt, then gradually add milk . Roll the dough into small, crack-free balls. Heat oil or ghee in a deep pan over medium heat. Carefully fry the balls until golden brown. In a separate pan, boil sugar and water to make syrup. Remove fried jamun and soak them in the warm syrup . Take out soaked jamun, allowing excess syrup to drain. Enjoy your homemade Gulab Jamun as a delectable dessert.
